Three eighths, and one quarter.
The quarter is bigger — but eighths fit right inside it.
A quarter is the same as two eighths.
So we slice only the quarter — the three eighths stay just as they are.
Now every piece is an eighth.
Three eighths plus two eighths…
…makes five eighths.
