Babushka sets out three plates.
Four pelmeni on the first plate.
The same four on the next plate — every plate gets the same.
And the same four again. Three equal plates of four.
Four, plus four, plus four — add the group again and again.
Three groups of four is three times four — twelve. Count the groups, not the numbers.
