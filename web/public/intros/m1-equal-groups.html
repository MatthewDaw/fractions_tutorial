Babushka sets out three jars.
Four plums in the first jar.
The same four in the next jar — every jar gets the same.
And the same four again. Three equal jars of four.
Four, plus four, plus four — add the group again and again.
Three groups of four is three times four — twelve. Count the groups, not the numbers.
