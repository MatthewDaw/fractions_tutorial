4×6=24
Loose buns all over — too messy to count.
Tidy them into a tray: four rows of six.
Count by rows — six, twelve, eighteen, twenty-four.
Spin the tray: four rows of six, or six rows of four — still twenty-four.
Score it: four sixes is four fours and four twos — 16 + 8 = 24.
A tray is a rectangle, povaryonok — rows times columns.
