What's on screen (region · affordance · note)

goal cue — goal · spoken · "Tidy six eighths" voiced aloud and shown on screen as a caption. No separate non-reader channel.
messy_bar — play · selectable · a bar made of many small equal pieces (6/8). The thing to tidy.
fuse_tool — play · tappable · pick a group size K; the room's signature verb. Even fuse snaps, uneven bounces back. The ONE active room tool.
height_guide — play · static · fixed accent line: the total amount never changes as pieces fuse → the single emphasis target.
