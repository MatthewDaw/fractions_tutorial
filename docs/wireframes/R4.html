What's on screen (region · affordance)

goal cue — "nine sevenths" voiced + shown as text (caption). No separate non-reader channel.
overflow stack — 9 sevenths, taller than one whole = the improper fraction. draggable, measured to the ruler.
ruler — marked in wholes; one full length = one whole. The measuring reference (no cups, no liquid).
crossing one whole — the overflow passing the whole-mark on the ruler. The teaching target → emphasis.

 

whole_unit_row — a segment that reaches a whole locks off + slides here; the next whole begins. drop_target.
leftover_tray — the 2 pieces over the last whole = the fraction part. Empty at an exact whole (trap).

check · progress · tutor_guide — the one persistent HUD action (≥60px), quiet progress, corner guide that speaks the goal.
